  • June 10, 2023 - New plugin in /advanced

    The "Suspicious Detection" plugin has been added to the /advanced command, alerting you if

    a user has one of Discord's 5 default avatars.

    Indeed, most scammers don't take the time to customize their avatar, which can be a sign of a suspicious user.

    However, this doesn't necessarily mean that the user is a scammer!

    This plugin is intended to be preventive and informative, not punitive!

  • May 12, 2023 - New commands

    /purge is now available, you can provide a number of messages to delete between 1 and 100 and also provide a specific user.

    For example, /purge 10 @chr will delete the last 10 messages from @chr#4627 and /purge 10 will delete the last 10 messages.

    /access is now available, you can provide a channel or category to lock / unlock (note that if you create a new channel and execute directly after the command, the bot will not have time to change the Discord cache, so you have to wait).

    A large number of channels to lock will cause lag and this could cancel the process.
